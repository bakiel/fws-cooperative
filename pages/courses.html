How It Works

Every educator. Every subject. One cooperative.

FWS courses are created by cooperative educators - people who teach, who care, and who know their subject deeply. Each course is a 10-video series designed for independent learners.

Purchase any course for lifetime video access - watch at your own pace, replay as many times as you need.
Subscribe monthly to unlock both the full course library and tutoring booking access with all FWS educators.
Once subscribed, book live tutoring directly from any educator's availability calendar for 1-on-1 sessions.

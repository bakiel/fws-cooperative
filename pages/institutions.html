ESI Details

How the Educator Support Initiative works

Two types of ESI educators - both available to member institutions.

Short-Term Coverage
Substitute Educators
Substitute educators post their availability on the FWS calendar. When you need coverage, you request a substitute and FWS matches you with an available, qualified educator.
Rate structure: Hourly rate applies for sessions up to 2 hours. A daily rate applies for sessions running longer than 2 hours. Rates are set cooperatively.
Semester Commitment
Regular Educators
Regular educators commit to a full semester contract with your institution. They teach on the same days and times each week, providing your program with stability and continuity.
Absence policy: If a regular educator is absent, a financial penalty is applied. FWS uses this to fund an FWS substitute so your program is never left without coverage.

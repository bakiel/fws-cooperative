Our Story

Built by educators,
for every learner

Family World School Cooperative was founded with one purpose: to make world-class education accessible to every independent learning program, family, and cooperative - regardless of location.

We are a nonprofit-backed cooperative, part of the It's My Miracle and DCA Network, working with international educators, curriculum designers, and academic authors to build the future of independent education.

"Working collaboratively to achieve shared goals with members and partners - building the future of independent education, one family and one community at a time." Family World School Cooperative Mission

What We Stand For

Our core values

I
Independence
We champion independent learning programs - home schools, private cooperatives, and family-run institutions deserve the same quality support as any institution.
C
Cooperation
Every educator, institution, and family that joins FWS contributes to a shared mission. We are stronger together than apart.
E
Excellence
Every FWS educator brings expertise, structure, and commitment. We hold ourselves and our community to a high standard in everything we do.
A
Access
Quality education should not be limited by geography, resources, or institutional affiliation. FWS is built to break down those barriers.

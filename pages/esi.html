Track B

Regular Educators - Semester Contracts

When an institution needs more than a one-off substitute, ESI's regular educator track provides a semester-long teaching relationship. The educator becomes a familiar face - building rapport with students and deep knowledge of the institution's curriculum approach.

This is ideal for independent schools running structured programs, home school cooperatives with recurring group sessions, and learning centers offering consistent subject support.

Requirements

Who Can Join ESI

FWS maintains quality standards to protect institutions, students, and educators alike.

Teaching Qualification

A teaching degree, diploma, or recognised alternative credential in your subject area is required for ESI enrollment.

Stable Internet + Device

Reliable high-speed internet and a device capable of video conferencing is required for all virtual delivery.

Background Check Consent

All ESI educators consent to a background verification process before activation in the network. Protecting our learners is non-negotiable.

Availability Commitment

Substitute educators must update their calendar weekly. Regular educators must commit to a full semester schedule before contract signing.

Agreement to FWS Terms

All educators sign the FWS Educator Agreement covering conduct, rates, booking protocol, and absence policies.

Platform Proficiency

Comfort with Zoom or Google Meets is essential. VEDAMO training will be provided for regular educators in Phase 2 of the program.

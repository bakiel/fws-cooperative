Our Mission

A cooperative built for independent educators and learners

Family World School Cooperative supports independent educational programs - including private non-government schools, home-school cooperatives, and family homeschools - with the educators, tools, and structure they need to thrive.

We are part of the It's My Miracle nonprofit and the Miraculous Transitions and DCA Network, working collaboratively with international educators, academic authors, and curriculum designers.

Getting Started

How FWS works

From sign-up to teaching or learning - it takes four simple steps.

1
Choose Your Role
Sign up as an Educator, an Institution, or an Individual Member. Each path has its own tailored onboarding.
2
Sign Your Contract
Contracts are completed and signed online. Regular educators are assigned semester schedules. Substitutes post availability on the calendar.
3
Teach or Learn
Educators deliver classes via Zoom or Google Meets. Members access courses and book tutoring. Institutions request and manage their educators seamlessly.
4
Grow Together
Track earnings, course purchases, and session history. The cooperative grows stronger with every educator and family that joins.

ESI Program

Educator continuity for every classroom

The Educator Support Initiative ensures your learning program never misses a beat. Whether you need a substitute for a day or a consistent weekly educator for an entire semester, FWS connects you with qualified professionals.

Short-Term
Substitute Educators
Post availability on the calendar. Hourly rate up to 2 hours, daily rate thereafter. Immediate classroom coverage when needed.
Long-Term
Regular Educators
Semester-long contracts with consistent weekly schedules. Absence penalties fund automatic substitute coverage for your institution.

Frequently asked questions

Who can book a session?
Any FWS member can book a tutoring session. Individual members with an active monthly subscription can book directly from educator availability. Institutions and prospective educators can book a consultation call.
How long are sessions?
Standard tutoring sessions are 60 minutes. Educator consultation calls are 30 minutes. For ESI substitute bookings under 2 hours, hourly rates apply. Daily rate applies thereafter.
What platform will the session be on?
All FWS sessions are delivered via Zoom or Google Meets. After booking, you will receive a confirmation email with the meeting link for your session.
Can I cancel or reschedule?
Yes. Sessions can be rescheduled or cancelled up to 24 hours before the start time without penalty. Contact us via the FWS contact page if you need assistance.
How do I become a member to access tutoring?
Purchase any FWS course or subscribe to a monthly tutoring plan. Once subscribed, you can browse educator availability and book sessions directly through the booking calendar.

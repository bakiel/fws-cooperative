Our Story
What is FWS?
Family World School Cooperative is a learning support cooperative for independent education programs - serving home schools, private non-government schools, and cooperative learning groups. FWS operates under the umbrella of the It's My Miracle nonprofit, aligned with the Miraculous Transitions initiative and the DCA Network.

FWS was built to solve a real problem: independent education programs are growing rapidly, but they lack the institutional infrastructure to access quality educators on demand, offer structured courses, or provide consistent learning support. FWS bridges that gap - connecting world-class educators with the families and communities that need them most.
Mission Statement

"To connect world-class educators with families and independent learning communities - making high-quality education accessible, flexible, and alive wherever learners are."

Colour Usage Guidelines

Crimson - Primary CTAs, brand marks, key highlights. Use sparingly for maximum impact.
Deep Navy - Primary page backgrounds and navigation. The dominant dark tone.
Heritage Gold - Eyebrows, borders, premium indicators. Never overuse - it signals excellence.
Warm Cream - Primary text on dark backgrounds. Warmer than white - more scholarly.
Navy Surface - Cards and elevated panels within dark-background sections.
Cool Grey - Secondary labels, metadata, muted descriptions. Always paired with stronger elements.

Never use these fonts

Inter, Roboto, Arial, Open Sans, Poppins, Montserrat - these carry no distinctive voice. FWS deserves a scholarly, warm identity.

Contrast principle

Cormorant Garamond for emotion and hierarchy. DM Sans for clarity and function. Two fonts only - always.

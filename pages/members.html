How Tutoring Works

From subscription to session in four steps

Private tutoring with FWS educators is simple. Subscribe, browse, book, and learn - all within the cooperative.

1
Purchase a Course or Subscribe
Buy any FWS educator course, or activate a monthly subscription to unlock tutoring booking.
2
Browse Educator Availability
FWS educators mark their available tutoring slots on their calendar. Browse and choose a time that works for you.
3
Book Your Session
Confirm your booking through the FWS platform. You receive a Zoom or Google Meets link instantly.
4
Learn Live
Attend your live 60-minute session. Ask questions, get guidance, and grow with your educator.
